TVA-Analytics Enablers [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Analytics Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging the Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue and the Analytics Enablers. The Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue is a Diagram Model-group ABB that encompasses the Data Analytics Business Service ABB. This service provides digital public services related to data analytics. The Analytics Enablers is a Grouping ABB that refers to components and services that serve any current or future Digital Service Infrastructure (DSI) requiring data analysis capabilities. This grouping includes several Application Service ABBs: Big Data Analytics, Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, and Smart Queue Management. The Big Data Analytics Application Service refers to a software application or platform that leverages advanced analytics techniques to process and analyze large and complex datasets, commonly known as big data. It enables organizations to derive valuable insights, identify patterns, trends, and correlations, and make data-driven decisions. The service typically includes data ingestion, storage, processing, and visualization capabilities, as well as advanced analytics techniques such as machine learning, natural language processing, and predictive modeling. The Data Analytics ABB is an Application Service that enables the use of procedures to analyze data and extract insights that can be used to improve business decision-making, optimize business processes, and drive innovation. The Business Intelligence Application Service ABB is another component of the Analytics Enablers, providing business intelligence capabilities. Lastly, the Smart Queue Management Application Service ABB is part of the Analytics Enablers, providing smart queue management capabilities. The motivation behind this view is to provide a comprehensive framework that leverages the capabilities of the Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue and the Analytics Enablers to enhance data analysis capabilities and support data-driven decision-making in the organization.
