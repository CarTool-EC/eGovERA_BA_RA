Commission Recommendation 87/598/EEC of 8 December 1987 ()
Description: The European Code of Conduct relating to electronic payment is a set of guidelines and principles established by the European Commission to ensure fair and transparent practices in electronic payment services. It aims to protect consumers by promoting secure and efficient payment systems, enhancing competition, and preventing fraud and abuse. The code encourages payment service providers to provide clear information to consumers regarding fees, charges, and terms of service, as well as ensuring the security and privacy of personal data. It also promotes interoperability and accessibility of payment systems across the European Union.
