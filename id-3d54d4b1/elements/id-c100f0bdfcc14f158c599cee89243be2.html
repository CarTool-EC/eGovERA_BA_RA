dct:descriptionThe Recommendation on Building a Joint Cyber Unit is a document issued by the European Commission on June 23, 2021. It proposes the establishment of a Joint Cyber Unit to enhance the European Union's cybersecurity capabilities. The unit aims to strengthen cooperation and coordination among member states, EU institutions, and relevant agencies in preventing, detecting, and responding to cyber threats. It emphasizes the importance of sharing information, expertise, and best practices to effectively address cyber risks and incidents. The recommendation also highlights the need for adequate resources, training, and technological advancements to ensure the unit's effectiveness in safeguarding the EU's digital infrastructure and critical systems.
