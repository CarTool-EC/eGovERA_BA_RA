Workflow Management ()
Definition: A Workflow Management Application Service, also known as Orchestration Application Service, refers to a software solution or service that enables the design, execution, and coordination of workflows within an organization. It provides a centralized platform for modeling, automating, and monitoring business processes, ensuring efficient and effective workflow execution. The Workflow Management Application Service typically includes features such as workflow design and modeling, task assignment and tracking, process monitoring and optimization, exception handling, and integration with other systems and applications.
