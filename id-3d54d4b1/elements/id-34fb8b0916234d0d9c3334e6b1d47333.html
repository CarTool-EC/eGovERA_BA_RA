Cyber Solidarity Act Proposal ()
Description: The proposal aims to strengthen the European Union's ability to detect, prepare for, and respond to cybersecurity threats and incidents. It outlines measures that member states must take to improve their cybersecurity capabilities, including establishing national cybersecurity centers and developing incident response plans. The proposal also establishes a European Cybersecurity Competence Center to coordinate research and development efforts and promote cybersecurity skills and knowledge across the EU. The regulation emphasizes the importance of cooperation and information sharing between member states and with the private sector to enhance the EU's overall cybersecurity resilience.
