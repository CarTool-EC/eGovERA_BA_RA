Blockchain policy ()
Definition: A Blockchain policy, as a specialization of a Data policy, can be defined as a set of rules, guidelines, and controls that govern the management, access, and utilization of data within a blockchain network or ecosystem. It outlines the specific protocols, procedures, and requirements for storing, validating, and securing data on the blockchain, ensuring integrity, immutability, and transparency.
