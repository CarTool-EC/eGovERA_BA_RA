Machine learning ()
Definition: Machine Learning is an application service within the field of Artificial Intelligence that enables systems to automatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. It involves the development and deployment of algorithms and models that can analyze large volumes of data, identify patterns, and make predictions or decisions based on the learned patterns.
