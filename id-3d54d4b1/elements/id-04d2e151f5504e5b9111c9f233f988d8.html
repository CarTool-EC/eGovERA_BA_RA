Regulation (EU) 2018/1724 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 2 October 2018 ()
Description: Regulation (EU) 2018/1724, also known as the Single Digital Gateway Regulation, aims to establish a unified digital gateway that provides easy access to information, procedures, and assistance services for citizens and businesses across the European Union. The regulation seeks to simplify and streamline the process of accessing public services and information online, making it more user-friendly and efficient. It also amends the existing Regulation (EU) No 1024/2012 to align with the new provisions. The Single Digital Gateway is an important step towards creating a more digital and interconnected Europe.
