Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 19 October 2022 ()
Description: Regulation (EU) 2022/2065, also known as the Digital Service Act (DSA), updates the European Union's legal framework for illegal content on intermediaries, in particular by modernising the e-Commerce Directive adopted in 2000. In doing so, the DSA aims to harmonise different national laws in the European Union that have emerged at national level to address illegal content.
