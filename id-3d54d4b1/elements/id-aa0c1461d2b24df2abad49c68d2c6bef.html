Regulation (EU) 2018/644 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 18 April 2018 ()
Description: Regulation 2018/644, also known as the Regulation on cross-border parcel delivery services, is a legislation enacted by the European Parliament and Council on 18 April 2018. The regulation aims to improve the transparency, affordability, and quality of cross-border parcel delivery services within the European Union. It introduces measures to enhance price transparency, increase competition, and provide consumers with more information about delivery options and prices. The regulation also establishes a regulatory framework to address issues related to access to essential facilities and the resolution of disputes between service providers. Overall, it seeks to promote a more efficient and competitive market for cross-border parcel delivery services in the EU.
