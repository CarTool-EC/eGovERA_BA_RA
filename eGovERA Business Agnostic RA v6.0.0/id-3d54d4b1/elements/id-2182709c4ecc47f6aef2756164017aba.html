Sovity Dataspace Connector ()
Description: A software component that enables data sharing and data sovereignty based on the EDC framework and the IDS standard. Additional information: The Sovity Dataspace Connector is an extension of the EDC Connector, which provides a framework for sovereign, inter-organizational data exchange. Sovity adds features such as easy access, automated integration, simple usage, fully managed services, and data space expertise to offer enterprise-ready solutions for data sharing. Sovity also provides different editions of the connector, such as the Community Edition, which contains pre-configured open source extensions. Example: An example of the Sovity Dataspace Connector in action is a consortium of healthcare providers sharing patient data securely and efficiently. The connector enables the seamless exchange of patient records, medical images, and diagnostic reports between hospitals, clinics, and research institutions. It ensures that sensitive patient information is protected and accessed only by authorized entities, while allowing healthcare professionals to collaborate and make informed decisions based on comprehensive and up-to-date patient data. The connector also facilitates the integration of data from wearable devices, remote monitoring systems, and electronic health records, enabling a holistic view of patient health and supporting personalized medicine initiatives.
