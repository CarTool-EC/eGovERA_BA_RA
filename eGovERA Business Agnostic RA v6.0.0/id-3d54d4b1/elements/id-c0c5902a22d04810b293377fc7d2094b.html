Regulation (EU) 2018/1807 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 14 November 2018 ()
Description: Regulation 2018/1807, also known as the "Regulation on the free flow of non-personal data in the European Union," was adopted by the European Parliament and Council on November 14, 2018. The regulation aims to establish a framework that enables the free movement of non-personal data across EU member states and prohibits unjustified data localization requirements. It seeks to promote the development of the digital economy by facilitating data sharing and portability, ensuring fair competition, and fostering innovation. The regulation also includes provisions for data protection and security, ensuring that the free flow of data does not compromise privacy or cybersecurity.
