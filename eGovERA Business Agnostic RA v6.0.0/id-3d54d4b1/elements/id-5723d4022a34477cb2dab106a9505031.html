eIDAS-Proxy-Service ()
Description: An eIDAS-Service operated by the Sending Member State and providing personal identification data. The eIDAS Service enables individuals and businesses to use their electronic identities (eIDs) to access online services, sign electronic documents, and authenticate themselves in digital transactions.
