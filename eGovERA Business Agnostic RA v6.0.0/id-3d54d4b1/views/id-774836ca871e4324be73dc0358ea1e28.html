TVA-Digital Workplace Enablers [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Digital Workplace Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging the elements of the Business Agnostic Digital Workplace Business Services and Digital Workplace Enablers. The Business Agnostic Digital Workplace Business Services is a Diagram Model-group ABB. It is designed to be universally applicable across various business domains. Within this model group, there is a Business Service ABB named Digital Collaboration. This service is described as a Digital Collaboration Digital Public Service, indicating its role in facilitating digital interaction and cooperation in the public sector. On the other hand, the Digital Workplace Enablers is a Grouping ABB. This grouping refers to components or services that enable the collaboration of work aspects while ensuring security and privacy. Within this grouping, there is an Application Service ABB also named Digital Collaboration. This service, while sharing the same name as the one in the Business Agnostic Digital Workplace Business Services, is distinct in its function and application. The relationship between these elements is that they are all part of the overarching TVA-Digital Workplace Enablers [Motivation] view. They are interconnected in their shared goal of enabling digital collaboration in the workplace, albeit through different means and in different contexts. The motivation behind these elements and their relationships is to create a comprehensive framework that leverages digital technologies to enhance collaboration and productivity in the workplace, while also ensuring the security and privacy of all involved.
