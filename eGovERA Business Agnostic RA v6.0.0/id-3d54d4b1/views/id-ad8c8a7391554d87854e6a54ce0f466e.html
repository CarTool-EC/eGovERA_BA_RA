TVI-Analytics Infrastructure Enablers [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVI-Analytics Infrastructure Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - infrastructure and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging the elements of the Analytics Infrastructure Enablers and Analytics Enablers. The Analytics Infrastructure Enablers is a Grouping ABB that refers to the set of components and services that support the development and deployment of data analytics solutions. This group includes four Technology Service ABBs: Analytics, Business Intelligence, Databricks, and Data Catalog. The Analytics ABB is a Technology service that enables the procedures of analysing a great amount of raw data to make conclusions about specific information. The Business Intelligence ABB is a Technology Service that enables the procedure of collecting, storing, and analysing the data produced by the public administration to improve and optimise decisions and performance. The Databricks ABB is a Technology Service that enables the procedures of a collaborative workspace to process large amounts of public administration data. The Data Catalog ABB is a Technology Service that enables the procedures of a centralised metadata repository to provide information about the data assets in a public administration. The Analytics Enablers is another Grouping ABB that refers to components and services that serve any current or future Digital Service Infrastructure (DSI) requiring data analysis capabilities. This group includes five Application Component and Service ABBs: Data Analytics, Big Data Analytics, Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, and Smart queue management. The Data Analytics ABB is an Application Component that enables the implementation of procedures to analyze data and extract insights that can be used to improve business decision-making, optimize business processes, and drive innovation. The Big Data Analytics Application service refers to a software application or platform that leverages advanced analytics techniques to process and analyze large and complex datasets, commonly known as big data. It enables organizations to derive valuable insights, identify patterns, trends, and correlations, and make data-driven decisions. The service typically includes data ingestion, storage, processing, and visualization capabilities, as well as advanced analytics techniques such as machine learning, natural language processing, and predictive modeling. The Data Analytics ABB is an Application Service that enables the use of procedures to analyze data and extract insights that can be used to improve business decision-making, optimize business processes, and drive innovation. The Business Intelligence and Smart Queue Management are both Application Services with unspecified descriptions in this context. The motivation behind these elements and their relations is to provide a comprehensive framework that supports the development, deployment, and enhancement of data analytics solutions, enabling better decision-making, business process optimization, and innovation within the public administration.
