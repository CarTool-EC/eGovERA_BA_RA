Organisational view [Motivation] ()
Narrative: The ‘Organisational view – DBCs, DPS and Information” viewpoint extends the organisational view and encompasses the realization of digital business capabilities through the deployment of digital public services that interact with specific information bases. Digital business capabilities represent the technological functions and capacities required to deliver services in a digital environment. These capabilities are leveraged by digital public services, which are designed and implemented to facilitate the delivery of services to users in a digital format. These digital public services rely on accessing and utilizing specific information bases to retrieve and process relevant data. By integrating digital business capabilities, digital public services, and information access, organizations can effectively harness technology to enhance service delivery, streamline processes, and meet the needs of users in the digital realm.
